Our team is made up of people who joined Sana from world-class technology companies and institutions. Together we’re improving the function of an entire, $5 trillion industry that directly affects hundreds of millions of people’s lives every day.

Our Ethos

We are for the creative spirits. The brilliant. The thoughtful. The audacious. The overachievers, the independent thinkers. The fighters and the true believers. These are the bright minds we hire. They’re extremely rare, but we’re ecstatic when we find them. They join Sana because they want their work to add up to something. Something big. A small team, we prefer a few incredibly focused people with diverse strengths, creating a collaborate hive mind.

Our Outlook

  • Customer First
    Focus on customer happiness and always put yourself in the customer's shoes. Aim to understand the customer's needs better than they do. Deliver a remarkable experience at every opportunity. Provide a consistent service, even as we grow.
  • Start With Why
    Break problems down to first principles and ask lots of questions. Challenge assumptions with data. Always strive to understand why customers care. Simplify.
  • Think Big
    Anticipate where we're going and invest in systems that scale – help us build for the future! Encourage crazy ideas, volunteer for impossible situations, and don't be afraid to go against the grain. Learn lots and grow quickly.
  • Courage
    Results matter, job titles don't. Do what you say. Respect others, but don't let bad ideas go unchallenged. Always be honest, direct and transparent. Tackle difficult conversations head-on.
  • Inspire
    Inspire confidence and admiration in every interaction someone has with Sana. Surprise and delight customers in clever and interesting ways. Execute tasks with an obsession for the details.
  • Move Fast
    Use processes to automate everything possible. Break rules when necessary. Don't ask for permission if you can stand firmly behind your decisions. Analyze failure and learn from it.

We value brilliant, audacious and thoughtful people.
