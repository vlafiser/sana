Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I estimate my MAUs?
    Sana considers any active logged-in user that has received a recommendation as a MAU. For example, a user may log in to your site in February, then continue to use your site in March without having to log in again. In this case, we would count this user as a MAU in both February and March.
  • What if I exceed my paid plan limits?
    Our plans are shaped to provide the flexibility you need to scale your infrastructure. You will be notified by email a few days before reaching the quota that is applied to your plan. You will be able to upgrade your plan anytime to fit your demands on the long term.
  • How long does it take to integrate Sana?
    The Sana platform is built around industry standard APIs which provides easy integration of deep learning algorithms. The architecture makes integration with any education system simple, and the platform can be production ready within days, not months.
  • What is the benefit of an annual plan?
    If you are on the monthly billing plan, your bill may fluctuate each month based on your user activity. The annual plan allows you to purchase MAU at a lower rate.
  • Who owns my data?
    You do. You retain ownership of all the data, we do not and never will share it with any third parties.
