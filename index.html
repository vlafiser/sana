AI for Education

Sana uses artificial intelligence to personalize educational content to each student. Within Sana Enterprise we partner with the world's largest and most innovative education companies and help them shape their digital future with Sana's technology.

As students learn with Sana,
Sana learns about them.

No two people learn in the same way. That's why Sana measures students answers, response times and an array of contextual information to figure out what they know, how they learn and how they forget. Building on these insights, our models help every student learn faster, and grasp ideas more easily. Sana makes learning so efficient, it practically feels like magic.

Just what your students need.
Right when they need it.

Sana models a student's learning state and predicts how they will perform on future interactions. Meaning that resources can be suggested to students based on their individual needs, and content which is predicted to be too easy or too hard can be skipped or delayed. The result is more engaged students, lower churn, and better learning experiences — for all.

Smooth implementation process.

Our API delivers blazingly fast performance with easy integration of machine learning models enabling you to build personalized learning experiences using just a few lines of code. To ensure you're always getting the most out of it, our Forward Deployed Software Engineers work as an extension of your team with end-to-end implementation including implementation reviews, UX workshops, and best practices.
