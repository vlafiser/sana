Meet Sana.

Sana measures the quality of your memories, how they change over time and uses what it learns about you to personalize learning. It then delivers information when you need it through clear, concise and interactive explanations. So you can learn more, in less time.

Enhanced learning.

Sana keeps track of your memories, and then uses its knowledge to remind you of salient points at just the right time. As a result, learning can be made up to x3 more efficient.

Intuitive explanations.

Make the abstract concrete by using simple, natural gestures to adjust values and see function transformations interactively. It's intuitive, beautiful math at your fingertips.

Scientific approach.

Sana is designed in collaboration with experts in neuroscience and based on extensive scientific research. Improving memorability, comprehension, focus, and more.

  • Review with smart notifications.

    Technology is most valuable when you don’t have to think about it. That's why we designed Sana to fit seamlessly into your life. Through smart notifications Sana gives you salient points at a glance.

  • Get individual feedback.

    During your session, Sana provides timely feedback — by letting you know when you’ve hit another milestone or tricks to get there, for instance — so you can stay motivated to reach your goals.

  • Follow the synapse.

    Sana schedules reviews when the synapse is red and repeats until the concept is stored in long term memory. By analysing your usage patterns over time, it schedules these reviews according to your daily life.

  • Go for the high score.

    After each session, you’ll receive a personalized Memory Score based on how well you answered questions. When you reach your personal bests or hit milestones, Sana is there to help you celebrate.
