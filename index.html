Personal down to the last element.

Every session is comprised of essential units of information, known as Elements, which include voice, sound, motion and interaction. Using Elements Sana evolves over time – optimizing the learning experience after each student.

Measures everything, even memories.

Memories play a central role in learning allowing students to retain knowledge and connect new concepts to comprehended ones. They also change in predictable ways over time which means Sana can measure them, and then remind students of salient points at just the right time.
